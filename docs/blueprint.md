# **App Name**: FarmAssist

## Core Features:

- Voice Query Input: Enables the app to receive a spoken question from the farmer in their local dialect and convert this to text using speech-to-text technology. This feature allows for hands-free operation and supports a wide range of local dialects to improve usability.
- Image-Based Diagnostics: Allows farmers to upload images of affected crops. The app will use AI to analyze these images, diagnose potential diseases or pests, and suggest actionable remedies. The AI model acts as a tool to help identify diseases and pests and provide appropriate advice.
- Market Price Data Integration: Fetches the latest crop market prices from public APIs, such as Mandi APIs, to provide farmers with real-time pricing information.
- Market Trend Analysis: Utilizing fetched market data, Gemini AI analyzes market trends to predict future price movements and provides actionable insights to farmers.
- Government Scheme Updates: Gathers the latest updates and information on government schemes and policies relevant to agriculture from government websites.
- Voice-Based Information Delivery: Converts analyzed data and insights into spoken language, providing farmers with information and advice via voice using text-to-speech (TTS) technology. Uses AI to choose the most salient data points for voice output.
- Continuous Query Support: Provides a user interface for farmers to ask further questions and seek more assistance. This helps address complex or follow-up queries to ensure comprehensive support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Green (#73A942) symbolizes growth, health, and nature, crucial for an app assisting farmers.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to offer a soft, neutral backdrop that doesn't strain the eyes during prolonged use.
- Accent color: Burnt orange (#CC6633) is used for calls to action and highlights, drawing attention to key functions and important information.
- Font pairing: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for the body text to ensure readability and 'Playfair' (serif) for headers to give an elegant feel.
- Utilize icons representing different crops, diseases, market trends, and government schemes to aid quick recognition and navigation.
- A clean, tab-based layout that is mobile-first. Intuitive design that arranges key features like disease diagnosis, market prices, and scheme info into easily accessible sections.
- Employ subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance the user experience, providing visual feedback and preventing the app from feeling unresponsive.
